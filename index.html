About

Who are you?

Professionally, I am a self-taught web developer exploring the world of JavaScript. Somewhere between web design and web development you can often find me. Had I known that mucking around with the innards of a website could be a career path, I would have taken MySpace page layouts a little more seriously.

Where did you come from?

I have a long history with Design. Growing up I loved to sketch and draw; with time it started to look less bad. As time went on I slowly found myself in the technical classes for graphic design, and grew into a better understanding of communication and marketing in college. Like every good college student, I landed far outside my field of design by working first for the United States Navy and second for an ecommerce sales team. I'm currently a sales manager, but learning all I can to explore the world of code.

What do you want?

To learn, to help, to discover more. I'm walking through the traditional ways of learning web development with self-paced online courses, and a collection of half-baked projects. There is plenty to work on and lots of things that interest me - did you know NASA just gives their APIs away? - But if there is an opportunity to grow, I'll raise my hand first.
